01 · Thesis

The next decade of outsized returns belongs to operators who put AI inside the physical world.

01

AI doesn't just accelerate. It unlocks.

Inside the right operator, AI workers do two things that compound. They accelerate the parts of the value chain that already work: pricing, ops, customer flow. They unlock parts that didn't exist: new products, new margins, new defensibility. The first is helpful. The second is what creates outsized returns.

02

We back companies that are hard to disrupt.

Logistics, healthcare, manufacturing, field services. Workflows the physical world or regulation makes hard to copy, hard to undercut, expensive to ignore. AI inside these workflows compounds. AI in front of a SaaS dashboard does not.

03 · How we invest

We work first. We invest second.

01

We are already inside.

Pre-deal
Forward-deployed engagement on the operating side. Months of building, embedding, watching the business change. By the time capital is on the table, we know the unit economics, the operating ceiling, and the people. Diligence is a formality.
02

Capital meets the inflection.

A growth plan. A tuck-in. A succession event. A recap. We underwrite from inside, not across the table.
03

The work and the capital compound.

Through the hold and beyond
The team stays embedded. The AI workforce keeps running. Workers built for one portfolio company become tools the next one inherits, on day one.
