01 · Thesis

The next decade of outsized returns belongs to operators who put AI inside the physical world.

01 / Artificial Intelligence

Models that compound.

Real value sits with operators who can deploy AI inside complex environments against a new operating truth. Every Gambit Capital company has a path to humans and AI workers building together from day one of our hold through exit.

02 / Physical Industry

Atoms that still matter.

Logistics, manufacturing, field services. Workflows where the physical layer makes the moat: hard to replicate, hard to undercut, expensive to ignore. AI inside these workflows compounds. AI in front of a SaaS dashboard does not.

03 / Regulated Markets

Rules that create scarcity.

Where the right to operate is the asset. Licenses, accreditations, and audited track records take years to build and a single breach to lose. We back the firms that treat regulation as a moat.
